Combined Type

The combined type includes symptoms of both inattention and hyperactivity-impulsivity. People with this presentation may have difficulty focusing and also show high activity or impulsiveness.

Common signs

  • Does not pay close attention to details or makes careless mistakes in schoolwork.
  • Has difficulty staying focused on tasks or activities.
  • Fidgets with or taps hands or feet, or squirms in seat.
  • Talks excessively or blurts out answers.
  • Often loses things necessary for tasks or daily life.
  • Gets easily distracted by extraneous stimuli.

In school this can look like…

  • Starting work but switching tasks frequently and not finishing.
  • Calling out or moving around during lessons.
  • Missing instructions and needing repeated clarification.

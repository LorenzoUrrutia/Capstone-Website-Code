About This Project

This website is a capstone project focused on building empathy and understanding around three commonly misunderstood learning disabilities: ADHD, Dyslexia, and Dyscalculia.

Through educational content and interactive simulations, this project aims to help people experience what it's like to navigate the world with a learning disability, and come away with greater awareness and compassion.

02

Why This Matters

Learning disabilities affect millions of people, yet they are still widely misunderstood. Misconceptions, like assuming someone with ADHD is just "not trying" or that dyslexia only means reversed letters, can lead to stigma and inadequate support.

This project was built to close that gap. Rather than just presenting facts, it invites you to step into someone else's experience through simulations and myth-busting quizzes.

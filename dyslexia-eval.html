Below is an Example of a Comprehensive Evaluation

① Online meeting: First, a licensed therapist meets with the child to guide a sequence of reading tasks

② Report and diagnosis: Then a 8-10 page report describes the test results of the meeting, any diagnosis, and suggested next steps, including recommended school accommodations.

③ Follow-up and action plan: Finally, a 30-minute follow-up with your a therapist reviews findings, answers questions, and outlines practical next steps and treatment or support options.

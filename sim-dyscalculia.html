Dyscalculia Simulation

Reflection

For many people with dyscalculia, numbers can feel unstable or unreliable. Even simple tasks may require extra effort because symbols, quantities, and positions can feel difficult to trust in the moment.

This simulation cannot capture every experience, but it models how uncertainty can appear even when the math itself is not complex.

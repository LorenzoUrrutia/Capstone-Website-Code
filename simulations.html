ADHD Simulation

In this simulation you will read word problems and experience distractions similar to those faced by individuals with ADHD. These distractions recreate the difficulty in using working memory, maintaining focus, and managing multiple tasks simultaneously. You can adjust the intensity level and enable or disable distractions to see how they impact task performance.

Dyslexia Simulation

In this simulation you will read different passages from familiar books. Letters will appear to shift and move to recreate the visual crowding and text distortion that individuals with dyslexia may experience while reading. You can adjust both the how much the text moves and word complexity to experience different reading challenges.

Dyscalculia Simulation

In this simulation, you will complete short number tasks where values and symbols feel slightly unstable. The math remains simple, but visual and symbolic uncertainty can create hesitation similar to what many people with dyscalculia describe.

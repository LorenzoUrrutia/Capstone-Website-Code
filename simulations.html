Dyslexia Simulation

In this simulation, letters appear to shift and move, recreating the visual crowding and text distortion that individuals with dyslexia may experience while reading. Commonly mixed up letters like "d", "b" , "p" , and "q" may be swapped. You can adjust both the how much the text moves and word complexity to experience different reading challenges.
